Common Questions

Everything you need to know

Quick answers to the questions we hear most often from our patients.

How long does a mobile hearing test take?
A full comprehensive hearing test typically takes 30-45 minutes. This includes a visual ear inspection, pure-tone audiometry, and speech testing. We allow up to 60 minutes for the entire visit so there's never any rush.
Do you accept my insurance?
We accept Medicare, BlueCross, UnitedHealthcare, Cigna, Aetna, and most major insurance plans. Contact our team with your insurance details and we'll verify your coverage before your first visit — no obligations.
What area do you serve?
We currently serve a 50-mile radius around the greater metro area, including most suburban communities. Enter your zip code when booking or give us a call and we'll confirm availability in your area.
Can a family member be present during the test?
Absolutely. We encourage family members or caregivers to be present during the evaluation. They provide valuable input about your daily hearing challenges and help you remember the recommendations afterward.
What happens if my hearing aids need adjustment later?
All our hearing aid fittings come with complimentary follow-up home visits for fine-tuning. Many adjustments can also be done remotely through our smartphone app, so you rarely need to wait for hands-on support.
