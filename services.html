Service 03

Safe Ear Wax Removal

Blocked ears can cause discomfort, muffled hearing, and even dizziness. Our clinical microsuction and irrigation techniques are gentle, effective, and provide instant relief in most cases.

  • Microsuction — the most comfortable and effective method
  • Warm water irrigation when clinically appropriate
  • Visual inspection via otoscope before and after
  • Immediate hearing improvement in most cases
  • Safe for perforated eardrums and existing ear tubes
Book Wax Removal Takes 15-30 minutes
