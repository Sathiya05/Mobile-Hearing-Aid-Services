Understanding hearing loss

Hearing loss affects millions of people, yet many wait years before seeking help. Understanding the types, causes, and treatment options is the first step toward better hearing.

Sensorineural Hearing Loss

Caused by damage to the inner ear or auditory nerve. Most common type, often age-related.

Conductive Hearing Loss

Blockage or damage in the outer or middle ear. Often treatable with wax removal or surgery.

Mixed Hearing Loss

A combination of sensorineural and conductive loss requiring comprehensive treatment approach.

How long does a home hearing test take?

A comprehensive in-home hearing test typically takes 45-60 minutes. This includes a thorough case history, otoscopic examination, pure-tone audiometry, speech testing, and a detailed discussion of your results and recommendations.
