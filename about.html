Our Mission

Making hearing care accessible for everyone.

At ClearSound, we believe that hearing health should never be compromised by geography, mobility, or busy schedules. Our mission is to eliminate the barriers between patients and the care they deserve.

We combine clinical excellence with the convenience of home visits, using state-of-the-art portable technology that matches or exceeds what you would find in a traditional clinic.

Our Vision

A world where hearing loss is never a barrier to connection. We envision communities where everyone has access to audiology care in the comfort of their own home.

Our Commitment

Every patient receives personalized, one-on-one care. We never rush appointments and we take the time to understand your unique hearing needs and lifestyle.

Our Journey

The ClearSound Story

From a simple idea to a movement changing how hearing care is delivered.

2016 — The Beginning

A Frustrating Experience

Our founder, Sarah Mitchell, watched her aging grandfather struggle to get to audiology appointments. The travel, waiting, and stress made him give up on his hearing health entirely. She knew there had to be a better way.

1
2
2018 — The Launch

First Mobile Clinic

After two years of planning, we launched our first mobile hearing clinic in Portland. Equipped with portable audiometers and a passionate team, we served 50 patients in our first month.

2022 — Expansion

Regional Growth

We expanded to cover 50+ cities across three states, adding tinnitus management and pediatric testing to our service offerings. Our team grew to 15 licensed audiologists.

3
4
2025 — Today

Trusted by Thousands

ClearSound is now the region's leading mobile hearing service, with over 500 5-star reviews and partnerships with major insurance providers. And we are just getting started.
