Styles

Find the right style for you

From nearly invisible to powerful behind-the-ear devices, we have a style to match your preferences.

Behind-the-Ear (BTE)

Classic power and reliability. The device rests behind your ear with a thin tube delivering sound into the ear canal. Ideal for moderate to severe hearing loss.

  • Long battery life
  • Easy to handle
  • Bluetooth streaming

In-the-Ear (ITE)

Custom-molded to fit inside your ear. Less visible than BTE and easy to insert. Great for mild to moderate hearing loss with dexterity considerations.

  • Custom fit
  • Discreet design
  • Advanced features

Completely-in-Canal

Our most invisible option. Sits deep inside the ear canal, completely hidden from view. Perfect for those who want discretion without performance.

  • Nearly invisible
  • Less wind noise
  • Natural phone use
